Thank you for taking the time to visit SKII'S BBQ AND WINGS. I have been a chef for 29 years. I learned how to cook at a young age of 5. Although I have attended college to study computers , food has been a passion of mine. I am qualified and certified to build websites while my other interest is to bring satisfying, tasting, and good quality food to the public. The food truck business has been an interest of mine for over 15 years. I know if you stopped by and tasted Skii's BBQ and Wings you will enjoy the flavors and savour the taste. Take the time to browse through the website. Let your mouth water and immediately get hungry. When you are done browsing through the menu, please order from the contact page or drop by to get yourself some mouth-watering BBQ Ribs and/or Fried Wings!!!
